Event Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
    Registration and Welcome
    Sign in at the university hall and collect your welcome kit
  • 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
    Opening Ceremony
    Introduction to the day's activities and theme presentations
  • 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM
    Mehendi & Photobooth
    Get traditional henna designs and take themed photos
  • 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
    Lunch Break
    Traditional wedding feast in the university cafeteria
  • 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM
    Group Activities & Games
    Participate in fun wedding-themed group competitions
  • 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
    Bride & Groom Ceremony
    Announcement of elected bride and groom with special performances
  • 5:30 PM - 8:00 PM
    Dinner & Dance
    Evening celebration with music, dancing and dinner
